Here are some of the projects I have worked on.

Over the past year I have been developing my digital artistry. I have worked as the digital artist for multiple group projects as well as the creative lead. A few of my works can be viewed in an online folder.
Rabbit Ears was a UX/UI case study myself and a group of cohorts collaborated on to help solve in the issue of keeping connected within reserves. We developed the idea of creating a tv based app that would host local news and events. Throughout the project we conducted interviews, created personas, and devloped HMW's to tackle this issue. We proudly and enthusiastically presented the case study to our fellow cohort and program instructers.
The North American House Hippos solve Zero Hunger was a project I participated in with fellow like minded individuals to come together and develop a solution to solve zero hunger within rural indigenous communities. Over the course of 6 sessions we worked within the Wavemakers metaverse to design and present our solution to our whole cohort and mentors. The House Hippos came up with the idea to create Aquaponic centres that were run by the communities. We gathered interviews, compared against compataters and compiled all this information in a slideshow that was presented.
